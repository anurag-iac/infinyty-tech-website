Fleet Ledger & Operator Settlement Portal

Automated Fleet Settlements & Compliance Hub

Challenge

A fleet management company relied on disconnected Google Sheets to manually reconcile driver payouts, vehicle rentals, and compliance records. This spreadsheet-based process was highly labor-intensive, error-prone, and caused frequent disputes.

Solution

Developed an automated Fleet Ledger Portal aggregating platform ride logs, auto-calculating settlements, tracking compliance documents, and providing a localized, dual-language interface (English & Hindi).

Business Impact

Replaced manual sheets with automated summaries, reducing settlement processing time from days to seconds. Decreased driver billing disputes by 80% and achieved 100% compliance visibility.
