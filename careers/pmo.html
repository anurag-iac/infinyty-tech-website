PMO (Project Management Officer)

Mumbai, Maharashtra, India (On-site) • Full-time • Client: Leading BFSI Company

Role Overview

This is a full-time on-site role for a PMO (Project Management Officer) for our client, a leading BFSI company based in Mumbai. You will be responsible for orchestrating multiple projects, tracking metrics, managing stakeholder alignment, and assisting executive leadership with program operations.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead and coordinate multiple cross-functional projects simultaneously, ensuring execution aligns with planned milestones.
  • Evaluate operational data, track project execution metrics, and provide clear actionable insights to stakeholders.
  • Formulate, manage, and analyze budgets and resource allocations to ensure projects remain within financial constraints.
  • Author and design presentations for executive leadership and corporate alignment.
  • Organize internal operational events, townhalls, and project syncs.

Required Qualifications

  • Years of Experience: 4+ years of experience in project management, program coordination, or PMO operations within enterprise environments.
  • Education: Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Finance, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Project Management: Strong baseline in project governance methodologies (Agile, Scrum, or Waterfall).
  • Skills & Competencies:
    • Proven experience in market research, business analysis, and strategy execution.
    • Exceptional communication skills to align teams and manage stakeholders across various corporate levels.
    • Proficiency with budgeting, resource allocation, and tracking financial metrics.
