Integration Engineer (Workato / n8n & Workday)

Remote • Contract / Contractor • Client: Leading Crypto Company

Role Overview

This is a hands-on technical role dedicated to designing and maintaining production-ready agentic AI workflows. You will join a leading crypto company on a contract basis, sitting within their IT/HR team. You will be responsible for orchestrating multi-step automations across a modern HR tech stack to measurably reduce manual effort and improve the employee experience.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design & Build: Create robust, multi-step agentic AI workflows in n8n or Workato to automate core HR processes like onboarding and job changes.
  • AI Orchestration: Implement tool-calling AI agents that take action by orchestrating LLMs and API calls.
  • Prompt Engineering: Apply advanced techniques (system prompts, tool design, guardrails) to ensure agents are reliable and safe for business-critical functions.
  • Systems Integration: Integrate with HR systems—primarily Workday—via APIs and webhooks.
  • End-to-End Ownership: Manage the full lifecycle of automation projects, from technical design to testing and impact measurement (KPIs).

Required Qualifications

  • Years of Experience: 6+ years in software engineering, systems automation, or integration engineering.
  • Agentic AI Expertise: Demonstrated experience building workflows that use LLMs for tool-calling and multi-step orchestration.
  • Automation Platforms: Expert-level skill in n8n and/or Workato.
  • Programming: Proficiency in Python, JavaScript/TypeScript, or Go for custom logic.
  • Security Mindset: Ability to design secure, auditable solutions suitable for handling sensitive PII in an enterprise environment.
  • Communication: Excellent skills to collaborate with non-technical HR stakeholders.

Preferred Skills

  • Full-stack development experience (React/TypeScript/Go).
  • Direct integration experience with Workday, Greenhouse, or Snowflake.
  • Familiarity with GenAI infrastructure (RAG, LangChain/LangGraph, LLM gateways).
  • Background in HR technology or People systems.
